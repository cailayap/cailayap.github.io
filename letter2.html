To the Man I am Spending the Rest of My Life With

Knowing you was already a blessing but having the opportunity to love you was a much bigger blessing. You are the love my life and I would be more than blessed to spend the rest of my life with you.

You are the man I have always dreamed of.

I have always dreamed of having someone that would understand me for who I am, would love me for who I am as well. I envisioned my future husband to be someone who always sees the good in others, and someone who values trust. A man who never forgets to keep her woman happy, a man who handles rough situations maturely is also a man I desired. And, my love, you are all that. You have shown me that you are capable of loving me even if I am hard to love. You have seen me at my worst and yet you still found a reason to stay and love me even more. We have our share of downturns, but you handle everything perfectly. You keep me happy, even if I am the moodiest person in the whole world, you never fail to turn each frown into a smile. And most importantly, you trust me completely with your whole life.

I promise to be the woman that you deserve.

I may not be perfect, but I try to be, and I promise to never stop trying. You deserve the world, love, and I will give that to you. I promise to be always by your side no matter what, I will always listen and understand you and your feelings. I promise to love you every second of every minute of every hour of every day.

You are my favorite everything.

You are my favorite person in the entire world, no one knows me like you do. I can be myself when I am around you and that is the best part. I am truly the happiest when I am with you. I truly believe that you are the man desired for me. I hope that you never get tired of loving me, because I will never get tired of loving you.
